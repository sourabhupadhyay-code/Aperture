Started on a two-burner stovetop.

Aperture began in 2019 in a one-car garage with a secondhand popcorn roaster and a kitchen thermometer. We kept the habit that started it all: writing down what we did, tasting what came out, and changing one variable at a time.

Today the roaster is bigger, but the method hasn't changed. We buy direct from three growing regions, roast in batches small enough to correct mid-course, and cup every batch before it's approved to sell.
